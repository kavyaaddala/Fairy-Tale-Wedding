MEET THE TEAM

We are a team of Italian wedding and event planners based in Florence, with a passion for travels, pizza, Spritz, aperitivo and of course weddings and events in our beloved Tuscany and Italy.

Irene is the owner and founder of Fairytale Italy Weddings.

She started working in the tourism business in 2008, while she was still studying Foreign Languages, Literatures and Cultures in Florence University (she can fluently speak English, French, Spanish and a little bit of Russian), when a hotel in Florence was looking for a receptionist and she said “yes”.

From that time, she has always worked in 4 and 5 stars hotels and in 2014 she became Events Manager in one of them.

She used to organize many private events, but weddings captured her attention: to her they are exciting, imaginative and unique, everyone is so different from each other.

02PROFESIONALITY

With more than 8 years of experience in the wedding industry, we’ll make use of our knowledge to organize an unforgettable wedding. We can assist couples like you to plan their big day, elopement or event, in the most stunning venues in Tuscany, whether you’re looking for a villa, castle, farmhouse or estate in the greenery of the Tuscan hills; however, we’re available to help you to organize your dream wedding throughout Italy.

Experience, knowledge of our territory, passion, attention to details are essential. Online you can find so many professionals, but we’ve made for you a selection of talented suppliers with high standards that can meet and go beyond your expectations in order to create charming atmospheres thanks to their flowers, decorations, lights, music…

04QUALITY

We’ll suggest trusted vendors to deliver the level of quality that you want for your big day. We can give you recommendations for every need and budget to provide you the most incredible settings and we’ll be pleased to take care of all the details and creative aspect to make your daydream wedding unforgettable.
